A programmer is a specialist who writes the source code for software. 
A programmer's job includes designing, developing, testing, and maintaining programs. 
The main programming languages are Java, Python, JavaScript, C++, and C#.
Programmers use algorithms and data structures to solve complex problems. 
Modern programming requires knowledge of frameworks, databases, and version control systems.
The profession of a programmer is in high demand in the labor market. Salaries in the IT sector
are among the highest. Programmers work in various fields: 
web development, mobile applications, artificial intelligence, data analysis.
